FAQS

What was your favorite class you took at SDSU?
My favorite classes at SDSU were Experimental Typography and UX Design. Experimental Typography allowed me to explore the creative possibilities of type, while UX Design taught me how to design with the user in mind,focusing on intuitive and engaging experiences. Both courses have greatly influenced my design approach.
What do you have planned for after graduation?
After graduation, I plan to work in the UX Design field, and in my free time, I’ll focus on freelance design projects. This will allow me to gain more experience and expand my portfolio while continuing to grow in the UX Design industry.
What work are you most proud of producing at SDSU?
The work I’m most proud of producing at SDSU is from my Experimental Typography class. I experimented with different materials and worked in analog, which allowed me to push the boundaries of traditional typography and explore creative, tactile design approaches. It was a great opportunity to break out of digital tools and focus on hands-on, innovative methods.
When will you be graduating from SDSU?
I am graduating from SDSU in May 2025 with a degree in Art and Graphic Design, focusing on Web Design and UX/UI Design.
